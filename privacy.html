Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 13, 2026

Overview

LiftScript is designed with your privacy in mind. We collect as little data as possible and never sell or share your information with third parties.

Data Stored on Your Device

Your workouts, saved workout files, and app preferences are stored locally on your device using Capacitor Preferences. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to share a workout.

Shared Workouts

When you use the Share feature, the workout text and a name you choose are uploaded to our database and assigned a random 6-character code. No personal information — no name, email, device ID, or IP address — is attached to shared workouts. Anyone with the code can view the workout. Shared workouts may be deleted periodically.

Analytics and Tracking

LiftScript does not include any analytics SDKs, tracking pixels, or third-party services. We do not track how you use the app, what workouts you log, or how often you open it.

Third-Party Services

LiftScript uses Supabase to store shared workouts. Supabase's infrastructure is hosted on AWS. No other third-party services are used. We do not integrate with advertising networks, analytics providers, or social media platforms.

Data Retention

Local data persists until you delete the app or clear your data. Shared workouts are stored indefinitely but may be pruned over time. There is no account system, so there is no personal data to retain or delete.

Children's Privacy

LiftScript does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Since we collect no personal data from any user, this applies equally to all users.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new date. Since we have no way to contact users (we don't collect emails), we encourage you to check this page periodically.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email support@liftscript.fit.
